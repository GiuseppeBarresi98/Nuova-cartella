Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries,and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetier topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart cranberries,and creamy brie.It will became your new fevorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions,thinly
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes(scape rind off with a paring knife if desired,thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method


1.Make the focaccia dought


In a medium-sized mixing bpowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the dough,thought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
